ABOUT HIVER

Committed to socialization, offering social media users opportunities to gather and engage in the true meaning of friendships, on and off the screens. Hiver is your app to simplify Hive (events/party) invites and planning! Whether you are looking to socialize or experience new things in your area, or planning a birthday party for a loved one, Hiver is for you!

OUR STORY

We started with the idea to simplify finding parties for college aged individuals, and slowly broadened our reach to give everyone the opportunity to be themselves through experiences everywhere.
A post covid lifestyle turned out to be less than fulfilling with social media taking the little joys of socialization away from us. Hiver is our solution to harnessing social media for genuine human connection.

OUR VISION

To create a social media environment for people to experience life's greatest joys of socialization.
