What is Hiverbee?

Hiver - Real Connection Starts Here

Find your people. Plan your vibe. Live beyond the scroll. Hiver is your new go-to app for discovering and planning real-life social experiences. Designed for young adults (ages 18-25), Hiver helps you beat loneliness, meet new people, and build genuine friendships — from farmers markets to late-night coffee hangs, worship nights to running clubs. Whether you're joining a Hive or hosting one, Hiver makes event invites, RSVPs, and planning effortless. No group chats. No pressure. Just real connection made simple.

What will I find on Hiver?

DISCOVER HIVES

Your new favorite running club? A worship night across town? A spontaneous open mic? Hiver curates events and communities around your location and interests — so you're always just one tap away from finding your people.

HOST A HIVE

Planning a birthday party, beach day, or casual hangout? Hiver makes inviting friends or the community simple. Set a vibe, pick a time, and boom — your Hive is live.

JOIN THE MOMENT

Tired of “maybe” RSVPs and dead group chats? Hiver centralizes everything so you can show up, vibe, and actually meet new people. No awkward planning or overthinking — just say yes and go.

BUILT FOR REAL CONNECTION

We're not here for swipes or passive scrolling. Hiver is designed to help you beat loneliness, ease social anxiety, and step into community — whatever that looks like for you.
